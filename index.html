About Me

Carrie is from a small town in Indiana known for the college where David Letterman scored his C average. She has an under-graduate degree from Purdue University in Mass Communication and a Master of Science from Ball State University in Information & Communication Sciences.

Carrie has an interest in creating her own small company in the future that specializes in niche Web Design services for small companies geared towards Community Service.

Carrie has spent the last ten years in the IT industry in various roles at a global Telecommunications company. She really enjoys the tech industry and is completing the DU Boot Camp in the interest of expanding her horizons.
